Development

The development of Org-babel is tracked on github/babel-dev. This repository contains:

  • lists of pending development tasks;
  • lists of known and resolved bugs; and
  • the Org-babel functional test suite, which is implemented as a large table and is run using Org-babel's spreadsheet functionality.

Please direct any questions, suggestions or comments to the Org-mode mailing list, just be sure to tag your subject line with "[babel]".
