REJECTED :results org should be org block

  • State "REJECTED" from "PROPOSED" 2009-12-22 Tue 14:40

The reason that :results org was introduced was to support seamless insertion of results into an org-mode buffer. This proposal would defeat that initial purpose. Also, the idea of org source blocks in an org-mode document seems needlessly complex and contorted. Of course I could be missing something here.

i.e. 

  #+begin_src org
    ,* whatever
  #+end_src
